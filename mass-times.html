Adoration · Adoración

First-Friday All-Night Adoration

On the First Friday of every month, the parish keeps watch with the Lord through the night.

  • 7:00 PM — Mass
  • Followed by All-Night Eucharistic Adoration
  • 7:30 – 8:30 PM — Confession during Adoration
  • Adoration continues until 8:00 AM Saturday Mass

All are welcome to spend an hour, or the night, in prayer before the Blessed Sacrament.

Getting here

Directions & parking

The church sits on Dauphine Street between Press and Montegut, in the heart of the Bywater. We are about a 10-minute drive from the French Quarter and easily reached by the Rampart-St. Claude streetcar line and several bus routes.

Parking

Street parking is available along Dauphine, Royal, and the surrounding neighborhood blocks. Please observe posted street-cleaning days. The church is wheelchair-accessible from Dauphine Street.

Plan your first visit

What to expect

Sunday Mass begins at 9:30 AM (English, with American Sign Language) and 11:30 AM (Spanish). The Saturday Vigil is at 4:00 PM. Come a few minutes early; greeters are happy to help you find a seat.

Dress is whatever you have. There is no parishioner card to flash — come in, sit anywhere, and pray with us. Children are welcome at every Mass.
